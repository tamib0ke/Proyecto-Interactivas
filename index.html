Hams

the game

Main Character

Hams is a hamster who loves strawberries. He
wants to reach his home, but to do so, he must
overcome various challenges and obstacles
along the way.

Assets

StrawberryCoin

This addition to the game will be
very helpful because if Hams loses
all his health, the strawberry will allow you to regain some.
It is useful for fighting mushroom enemies.

PowerUp

These little stars will help
you with different powers, each one with
a different ability that you'll discover when you collect it.
Make sure to grab as many as you can

Enemy

The evil mushroom will decrease
your health or could even kill you.
Be careful as they chase you and
can grow larger, blocking your path.
